Augmented Reality for Techs

As the augmented reality (AR) technology matures, more and more industries begin to recognize the incredible opportunities that this technology can offer in designing tools that can help their technical field teams as well as their customers to perform their related tasks more effectively. Combining the AR technology with the emerging Machine Learning platforms can further enable industries to create tools that could range from tools that can help in training new field technicians in use of new equipment and test sets to providing end customers applications which can guide them through installing new premises equipment in their homes.

  • Help in reduce training time for novice technicians or improve customer experience by reducing errors and calls to AT&T for help.
  • Helps in building new product launch, help in distance T3 help doing critical first response users
