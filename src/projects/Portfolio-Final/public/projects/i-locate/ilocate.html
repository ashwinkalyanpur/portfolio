iLocate

iLocate is designed to keep track of tech’s equipment and send instant alerts to techs and their managers, if any item is missing when the tech leaves the client’s premises. Techs are also able to perform a manual Bluetooth scan to get their truck’s inventory.

Desktop application tracks equipment and locations from live scans.

VTS gets auto udated and notifies mangers if techs change vehicles

Get Seamless alert for any event related to device allotted

Handheld device allows managers to scan beacons and create inventory for truck. Handheld devices triggers auto scan tracks on vehicle and equipment status.

Techs can track their past, current and next job location and are able to see nearby techs with specific tools or job skills in an interactive map.

  • Techs will not loose or misplace their equipment
  • Managers and techs can keep track of their inventory
  • Techs can engage or ask for help from other techs nearby
