CDOO Driven SMS

The CDOO Driven SMS project is a multi-application solution intended to reduce “No Access” dispatch completions by implementing a better communication process with the customer via SMS. On the day of a customer’s scheduled installation or repair job, an SMS message will be sent which will include a link to the TSNR application.

There, the customer will be able to view pertinent information about their appointment, including a link to provide a real-time update of the technician’s estimated arrival time at their home.

This will include:

  • A graphical user interface showing a progress bar illustrating the assigned technician’s status relative to the customer’s appointment.
  • An updated Arrival Time window based on the technician’s current status, location, and drive time to the customer’s home.
  • A real-time map showing the technician’s current location on his or her way to the customer’s home.
