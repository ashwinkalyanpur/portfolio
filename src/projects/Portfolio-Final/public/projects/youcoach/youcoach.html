youCoach

The you Coach Project will provide tools for the ATO Network Managers to completely overhaul their ability to coach, train, evaluate, recognize, and reward their technicians and agents. This will enable us to have the best technicians and agents in the industry serving our customers. It will leverage 10 years of standardized processes to deliver an integrated, homogeneous mobile platform. You Coach will use data in new ways to provide precise actions the manager can pursue with each technician to drive the right behaviors. You Coach is the evolution to predictive analytics and proactive coaching. It will:

  • Focus on Coaching
  • Include Employee Performance based cash awards for achieving performance goals
  • Proactive Coaching with Data
  • Simple to Understand
  • Intuitive
  • Simple & flexible metrics
  • Common and simplified goal methodology
  • Metrics & behaviors connected
  • Easy to Use
  • Integrated performance coaching plans, training and notification
  • Mobile platform for field personnel
  • Flexible administrative interface for clients to make updates and changes
  • Rethink Cost Structure
  • Developed & managed internally
  • Eliminate dependence on vendor
  • Improve accountability
