Artificial Intelligence Advisor

Artificial Intelligence Advisor is a diagnostic tool to detect and predict CPE anomalies based on historical CPE data. It has a Machine Learning Engine that runs on the Big Data platform and the larger Hadoop Environment.

  • A data driven platform that enables it to be a CPE prediction platform for multiple types of devices (STB, RG, ...)
  • Automated, config-based preprocessing pipelines enable CPE Advisor to iterate over various scenarios as quickly and as reliably as possible
  • GUI for verification purposes
  • Reduces unneeded CPE Swaps
  • Reduces dispatches for CPE Swaps
  • Reduces Reverse Logistics Costs
  • Improve Customer Experience by Alerting Customers before their device fails
