Rakuten International Carrier Platform (RIC)

Role
Senior UX Designer
Duration
10 Months
Team
Product Managers, Engineers, UX
Tools
Figma, Adobe XD, Jira

Designed telecom carrier onboarding workflows including bilateral agreements, SMS routing configuration and carrier integration flows.

Project Overview

Designed a telecom carrier management platform enabling global telecom operators to configure SMS routing, pricing models and network partnerships.

Problem

Carrier onboarding involved complex manual workflows and multiple configuration steps. The platform required simplified workflows for telecom operations teams.

My Role
  • UX research and workflow analysis
  • Wireframe and prototype creation
  • Stakeholder design presentations
Solution

Designed a unified interface for carrier onboarding, routing configuration and price list management.

Impact
  • Simplified telecom onboarding workflow
  • Improved operational efficiency
  • Reduced configuration errors
